About Me

Hi! I'm Peter Cooke

I'm a cinephile who likes to act and take photos. Recently, due to COVID-19, I have been unable to attend school, however I have sent my time watching Life on Mars and studying for my cisco entry exam. Although this may seem like pandering outside of work I enjoy programming and solving problems, currently I am using Edabit to make sure it stays in my head and to challenge myself to do better.

I am also a member of The Actors Workshop a youth theatre company which I have been attending for over 5 years putting on over 4 performances a year. This is a large part of my life which I am enjoying to go to and would like to continue to attend in the future.

I started being interested in photography in 2016 in margate when I took a photo of the sunset. It was the way the light hit the water in the photo I found it quite beautiful. I hope over these 4 years I have improved, I personally think I have but that first image always has held a soft spot in my heart even though it seems quite amature compared to some of the photos I have taken since. When I was sponsered to review Saal Digitals proffesion line photobook I chose that image the cover. I used to take photos with a Samsung J5 but now I use a Canon 1200D for most of the heavy work and a Google Pixel 3A for when I don't have my camera.
